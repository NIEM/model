biom:ToothID

element ToothID in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/5.0/

Definition

An identifier of a tooth. Teeth shall be numbered utilizing the permanent and deciduous teeth codes in ANSI/ADA Specification No. 3950.
