biom:DiscontinuityInformation

element DiscontinuityInformation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/5.0/

Definition

A description of whether there has been post-recording removal (cutting) of a signal or non-presence of a useful signal from the original recording of the acoustic vocalizations in a way that disrupts time references
