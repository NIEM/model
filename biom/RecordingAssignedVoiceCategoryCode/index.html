biom:RecordingAssignedVoiceCategoryCode

element RecordingAssignedVoiceCategoryCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/5.0/

Definition

A kind of first information item is the assigned voice indicator / AVI. It is mandatory if this field is used. It indicates if the voice recording sample was obtained from a known subject.
