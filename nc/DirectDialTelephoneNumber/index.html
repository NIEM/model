nc:DirectDialTelephoneNumber

element DirectDialTelephoneNumber in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/niem-core/4.0/

Definition

A telephone number consisting of a shortened sequence of numbers that can be used within a local telephone system, such as a particular network or a corporate phone system.
