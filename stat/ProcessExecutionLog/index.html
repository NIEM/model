stat:ProcessExecutionLog

element ProcessExecutionLog in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

An output of a process step which is not directly related to the transformed output it produced. It may include data that was recorded during the real time execution of the process step.
