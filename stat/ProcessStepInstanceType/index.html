stat:ProcessStepInstanceType

complexType ProcessStepInstanceType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

A data type for an executed step in a business process. A process step instance specifies the actual inputs to and outputs from an occurrence of a process step.
