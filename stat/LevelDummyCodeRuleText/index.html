stat:LevelDummyCodeRuleText

element LevelDummyCodeRuleText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

A rule for the construction of dummy codes from the codes of the next higher level (used when one or several categories are the same in two consecutive levels).
