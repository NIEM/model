j:SubjectMOActionFeature

element SubjectMOActionFeature in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A crime scene feature (i.e., object, picture, written word, sound, or particular mark that represents something else by association, resemblance, or convention), e.g., brand, tattoo, tag, graffiti, left behind by the subject, which the subject adopts as his signature or trademark behavior, gaining feeling such as satisfaction, remorse, or pride that allows him to justify his crime and fuel future acts..
