j:VisitorRequestListAssociation

element VisitorRequestListAssociation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.0/

Definition

A relationship between a subject and a person who has visited, has tried to visit, or has been designated by a subject as a potential visitor whether approved, denied, or pending approval by the facility.
