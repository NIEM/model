j:ConditionalReleaseCondition

element ConditionalReleaseCondition in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

A requirement or prerequisite for release. A restriction, modification, or qualification of a term or terms of release. (Ex. no contact with victim; do not leave jurisdiction of court, etc.)
