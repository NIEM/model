j:CrashVehicleOccupantsQuantity

element CrashVehicleOccupantsQuantity in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.0/

Definition

A total number of injured and uninjured occupants in this motor vehicle involved in the crash, including persons in or on the motor vehicle at the time of the crash.
