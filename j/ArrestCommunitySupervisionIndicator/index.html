j:ArrestCommunitySupervisionIndicator

element ArrestCommunitySupervisionIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

True if a person was under some kind of community criminal justice supervision, e.g., probation, parole, pre-trial services, day reporting, at the time of the arrest; false otherwise.
