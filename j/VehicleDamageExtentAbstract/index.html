j:VehicleDamageExtentAbstract

element VehicleDamageExtentAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

A data concept for an estimation of total damage to motor vehicle from crash. Disabling damage implies damage to the motor vehicle that is sufficient to require the motor vehicle to be towed or carried from the scene.
