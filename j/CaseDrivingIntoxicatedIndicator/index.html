j:CaseDrivingIntoxicatedIndicator

element CaseDrivingIntoxicatedIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

True if a case involves a person who was driving with blood alcohol level that is higher than the law allows; false otherwise.
