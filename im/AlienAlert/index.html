im:AlienAlert

element AlienAlert in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.2/

Definition

An alert for the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) if the alien has been formally removed from the U.S. or could pose a risk based on safety or other factors.
