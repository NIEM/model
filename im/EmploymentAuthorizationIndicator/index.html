im:EmploymentAuthorizationIndicator

element EmploymentAuthorizationIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.2/

Definition

True if the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) has authorized the alien to work for a United States business; false otherwise.
