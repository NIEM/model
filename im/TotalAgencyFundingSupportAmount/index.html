im:TotalAgencyFundingSupportAmount

element TotalAgencyFundingSupportAmount in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.0/

Definition

A number indicating the total funding amount provided by a US Government agency to the Exchange Visitor (EV), in order to support his/her participation within a designated EV Program.
