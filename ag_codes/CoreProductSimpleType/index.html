ag_codes:CoreProductSimpleType

simpleType CoreProductSimpleType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/ag_codes/4.0/

Definition

A data type containing classification codes of core agricultural products defined by the Pilot CVT (Crop Validation Table). Products are classified into individual crop and commodity types, tree types, and types of livestock.
