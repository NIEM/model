st:ThroughLanesNumeric

element ThroughLanesNumeric in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/surfaceTransportation/5.0/

Definition

A total number of through lanes on the segment. This excludes auxiliary lanes, such as collector-distributor lanes, weaving lanes, frontage road lanes, parking and turning lanes, acceleration/deceleration lanes, toll collection lanes, shoulders, and truck
