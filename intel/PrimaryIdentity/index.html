intel:PrimaryIdentity

element PrimaryIdentity in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/intelligence/4.0/

Definition

An identity that a person is first known as (e.g., for law enforcement), primarily known as, or primarily goes by. This may or may not be the -real- or authenticated identity.
