em:EventEmergencyDepartmentVisitsStatusText

element EventEmergencyDepartmentVisitsStatusText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/5.0/

Definition

A status of emergency department visits for an event, such as whether number of visits have increased or decreased compared to a previous reporting periods. The analysis may also include the the number of daily visits and the type of visits.
