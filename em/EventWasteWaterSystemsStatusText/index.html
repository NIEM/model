em:EventWasteWaterSystemsStatusText

element EventWasteWaterSystemsStatusText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/4.2/

Definition

A description of the status of waste water treatment plants and capacities in affected area. This data element is used for incidents caused by hurricanes, some chemical poisonings, or waterborne infectious diseases.
