em:EventEpidemiologicalInvestigationText

element EventEpidemiologicalInvestigationText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/5.0/

Definition

A set of information about the cause of an outbreak or incident as well as an interpretation of epidemiological data, findings from laboratory tests performed on environmental or clinical specimens, and recommendations for future actions.
