em:UncertaintyDistanceText

element UncertaintyDistanceText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/4.1/

Definition

A confidence measure derived from the number of cell sites, distance of the coordinates recorded from the cell site, accuracy of measurement of the coordinates and is typically expressed in meters.
