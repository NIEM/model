em:EventPopulationTestedQuantity

element EventPopulationTestedQuantity in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/4.2/

Definition

A total number of people in a defined population that are tested. For example, the group tested may be part of an outbreak or public health incident.
