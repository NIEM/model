em:EventImpactLevelAbstract

element EventImpactLevelAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/4.2/

Definition

A data concept for a level of impact (i.e., minimal, minor, moderate, major, severe) of an event based on the amount of disruption to normal activities and services.
