atf:ExplosiveComponentCodeSimpleType

simpleType ExplosiveComponentCodeSimpleType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/atf/4.0/

Definition

A data type for an item contained within an explosive device designed to cause harm or damage when propelled by an explosion.
