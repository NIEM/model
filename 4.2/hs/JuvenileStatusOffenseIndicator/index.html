hs:JuvenileStatusOffenseIndicator

element JuvenileStatusOffenseIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.2/

Definition

True if the alleged act is an offense when committed by a juvenile but not when committed by an adult; false otherwise. (i.e., acts such as truancy, violating curfew, running away from home, and the like are not offenses unless the offender is under the age of majority.)
