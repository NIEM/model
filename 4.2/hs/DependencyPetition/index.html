hs:DependencyPetition

element DependencyPetition in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.2/

Definition

A document that is filed with the Court, typically by an attorney representing the Child Welfare Agency, that formally files allegations of abuse and/or neglect against one or more alleged perpetrators.
