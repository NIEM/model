hs:childSupportEnforcementCaseIDStatusText

attribute childSupportEnforcementCaseIDStatusText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.2/

Definition

An attribute that shows the status of a IV-D case identifier assigned by a local agency to a child-support-enforcement case ("IV-D" refers to Title IV-D of the Social Security Act).
