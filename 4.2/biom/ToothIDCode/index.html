biom:ToothIDCode

element ToothIDCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/4.2/

Definition

A code to indicate the tooth number. Teeth shall be numbered utilizing the permanent and deciduous teeth codes in ANSI/ADA Specification No. 3950.
