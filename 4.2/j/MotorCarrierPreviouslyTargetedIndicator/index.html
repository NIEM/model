j:MotorCarrierPreviouslyTargetedIndicator

element MotorCarrierPreviouslyTargetedIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.2/

Definition

True if a motor carrier was targeted in the past, for increased enforcement action due to a poor safety record; false otherwise.
