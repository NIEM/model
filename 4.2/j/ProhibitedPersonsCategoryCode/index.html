j:ProhibitedPersonsCategoryCode

element ProhibitedPersonsCategoryCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.2/

Definition

A reason for the denial or the reason for a NICS Index submission. It can be one of the Brady Act categories or represent a Denied Person record submitted by a State.
