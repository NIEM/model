ag:GeospatialShapeModifiedOtherReasonText

element GeospatialShapeModifiedOtherReasonText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

A free form text providing the reason that a geospatial shape was modified from its original form when a reason other than the possible list of provided reasons is needed.
