ag:ProductPlantingCode

element ProductPlantingCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

A code identifying the planting of a product/crop as either conventional (planted as normal or no planting required) or unconventional/irregular in a predefined way.
