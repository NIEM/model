j:DriverLicenseCardIdentification

element DriverLicenseCardIdentification in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

An identification that is affixed to the raw materials (card stock, laminate, etc.) used in producing driver licenses and ID cards. The numbers are issued by the material's manufacturer and provide a unique reference to a card within a jurisdiction.
