j:VehiclePaperMCOIssuedIndicator

element VehiclePaperMCOIssuedIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

True if a paper copy of the manufacturer's certificate of origin (MCO) was sent from the manufacturer to the purchaser of the vehicle; false otherwise.
