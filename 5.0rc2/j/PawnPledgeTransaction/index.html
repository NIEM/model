j:PawnPledgeTransaction

element PawnPledgeTransaction in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

An inbound transaction when goods are taken as security for money lent. This includes goods received under a contract of sale where the seller has the right to buy back the goods.
