j:PawnItemReturnTransaction

element PawnItemReturnTransaction in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/7.0/

Definition

A transaction where items coming into the dealer's business have been previously processed by the dealer's business. An example would be for an item that is sold or consigned-out, and is later returned to the shop.
