st:RoadwayCategoryEndingRampTerminalCode

element RoadwayCategoryEndingRampTerminalCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/surfaceTransportation/5.0/

Definition

A ramp is described by a beginning and ending ramp terminal in the direction of inventory. This element describes the type of roadway intersecting with the ramp at the ending terminal.
