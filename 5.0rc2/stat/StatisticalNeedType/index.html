stat:StatisticalNeedType

complexType StatisticalNeedType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

A data type for a requirement, request or other notification that will be considered by an organization. A statistical need does not necessarily have structure or format - it is a 'raw' need as received by the organization. A statistical need may be of a variety of types including environmental change or information request.
