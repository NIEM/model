stat:ChangeEventTuple

element ChangeEventTuple in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

A record of which identifiable artifacts were changed by a change event. It keeps track of the source identifiable artifact(s) to which the change event was applied and the resulting target identifiable artifact(s).
