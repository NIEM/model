stat:ProcessSupportInputType

complexType ProcessSupportInputType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

A data type for a form of process input that influences the work performed by the process step, and therefore influences its outcome, but is not in itself changed by the process step.
