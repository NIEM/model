stat:OwnerType

complexType OwnerType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/statistics/5.0/

Definition

A data type for a statistical office or other authority responsible for defining, specifying, creating and making decisions about the maintenance of an information object and/or its metadata. Some information objects may have several Owners.
