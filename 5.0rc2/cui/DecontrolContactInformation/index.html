cui:DecontrolContactInformation

element DecontrolContactInformation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/cui/5.0/

Definition

A point of contact and preferred method of contact information for a Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) decontrolling event, to allow authorized holders to verify that a specified event has occurred.
