cui:DocumentSupplementalAdministrativeMarkingAbstract

element DocumentSupplementalAdministrativeMarkingAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/auxiliary/cui/5.0/

Definition

A data concept for a supplemental administrative marking (e.g., Draft, Deliberate, Predecisional, Provision) to inform recipient(s) of the non-final status of a document.
