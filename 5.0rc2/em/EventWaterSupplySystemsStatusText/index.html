em:EventWaterSupplySystemsStatusText

element EventWaterSupplySystemsStatusText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/5.0/

Definition

A description of the status and capacities of the water supply systems in affected area. This data element applies to incidents caused by hurricanes, some chemical poisonings, or waterborne infectious diseases.
