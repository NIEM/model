em:DonatedItem

element DonatedItem in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/emergencyManagement/5.0/

Definition

An item donated by a person or organization. Types include money, services, goods (e.g. clothing, toys, food, or vehicles), and for those medical needs (e.g. organs, tissue, or blood).
