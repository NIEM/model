hs:MedicalAssistanceContactPersonInformationDescriptionText

element MedicalAssistanceContactPersonInformationDescriptionText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/5.0/

Definition

A description of a person's contact information, used if Medical Assistance is processed by a different State Agency than the one that processes Food Stamps (FS) & Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF).
