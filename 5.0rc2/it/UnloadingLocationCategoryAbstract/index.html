element UnloadingLocationCategoryAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/5.0/

Definition

A data concept for a code to identify a seaport, airport, freight terminal, rail station or other place at which goods are unloaded from the transport means used for their carriage.
