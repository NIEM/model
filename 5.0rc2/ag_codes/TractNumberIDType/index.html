ag_codes:TractNumberIDType

complexType TractNumberIDType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/ag_codes/5.0/

Definition

A data type for identification number assigned to a unit of contiguous land that is under one ownership and that is operated as a farm or part of a farm.
