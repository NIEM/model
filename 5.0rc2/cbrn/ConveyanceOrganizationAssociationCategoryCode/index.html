cbrn:ConveyanceOrganizationAssociationCategoryCode

element ConveyanceOrganizationAssociationCategoryCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/5.0/

Definition

A description of the kind of relationship between a conveyance and organization. For example, an aircraft may have an owner, operator, lessee, etc.
