im:BinationalCommissionTotalFundingSupportAmount

element BinationalCommissionTotalFundingSupportAmount in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/5.0/

Definition

A number indicating the amount of funding provided to the Exchange Visitor (EV) by the Binational Commission of the Exchange Visitors home country, in order to support his/her participation in a designated EV Program.
