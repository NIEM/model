im:TwoYearGovernmentFinancialSupportIndicator

element TwoYearGovernmentFinancialSupportIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/5.0/

Definition

True if the Exchange Visitor (EV) is subject to a two year residence requirement due to receiving financial support from either the U.S. or EV's Government; false otherwise.
