im:AlienRecidivistIndicator

element AlienRecidivistIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/5.0/

Definition

True if the encounter is with a non-criminal alien who has a tendency to slip back into previously recorded criminal activities; false otherwise.
