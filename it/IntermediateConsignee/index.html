it:IntermediateConsignee

element IntermediateConsignee in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.2/

Definition

A transit (in-bond) movement, the identity of the intermediate consignee who may take possession of the goods from consignor to exporting carrier and from importing carrier to consignee.
