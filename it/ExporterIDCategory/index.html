it:ExporterIDCategory

element ExporterIDCategory in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/5.0/

Definition

A code for identification of party who makes, or on whose behalf the export declaration is made, and who is the owner of the goods or has similar rights of disposal over them at the time when the declaration is accepted.
