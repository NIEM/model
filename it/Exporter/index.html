it:Exporter

element Exporter in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.0/

Definition

A person who makes an export declaration or on whose behalf the export declaration is made. This is the owner of the goods or has similar right of disposal over them at the time when the declaration is accepted.
