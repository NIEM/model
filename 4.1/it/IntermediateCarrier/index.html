it:IntermediateCarrier

element IntermediateCarrier in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

Definition

A transit (in-bond) movement, the identity of the carrier's which transport the goods from consignor to exporting carrier and from importing carrier to consignee. A party providing the transport of goods between named points.
