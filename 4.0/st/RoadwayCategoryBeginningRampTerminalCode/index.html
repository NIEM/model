st:RoadwayCategoryBeginningRampTerminalCode

element RoadwayCategoryBeginningRampTerminalCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/surfaceTransportation/4.0/

Definition

A ramp is described by a beginning and ending ramp terminal in the direction of ramp traffic flow or the direction of inventory. This element describes the type of roadway intersecting with the ramp at the beginning terminal.
