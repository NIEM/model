im:DependentPrincipalDesignatedSchoolOfficialTerminatedM2Indicator

element DependentPrincipalDesignatedSchoolOfficialTerminatedM2Indicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.0/

Definition

True if a Principal Designated School Official (PDSO) or Designated School Official (DSO) terminated the aliens dependent status (M-2) to a vocational student; false otherwise.
