ag:TaxIdentificationCategoryCode

element TaxIdentificationCategoryCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

A type code identifying the type of identification assigned to a specific individual, business or other entity and is used to authenticate the individual/entity for matters of taxation and other government business.
