j:Hearing

element Hearing in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.0/

Definition

A proceeding before an administrative agency or authority to decide how to deal with failure to comply with rules, to determine an issue of fact, or to reach an administrative decision based on evidence.
