cbrn:CountDataValueList

element CountDataValueList in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.0/

Definition

A number indicating the counts accumulated during a measurement period over the entire energy range measured by a radiation detector or within pre-defined energy windows.
