cbrn:AnalysisConfidenceValue

element AnalysisConfidenceValue in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.0/

Definition

An indication of confidence, as a percent ranging from 0.0 to 100.0, in the overall accuracy of the analysis, where increasing values indicate higher confidence.
