hs:ServiceCategoryAbstract

element ServiceCategoryAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.2/

Definition

A data concept for a kind of a service that will be provided as part of a case plan. Categories include: assessment, treatment, program, benefit, education, transportation, respite care.
