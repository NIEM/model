hs:BiologicalParentDeterminationDescriptionText

element BiologicalParentDeterminationDescriptionText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.2/

Definition

An additional description of the details about the determination of a biological relationship between a putative parent and a child (for example, findings regarding the location and date of conception, or the results of DNA tests).
