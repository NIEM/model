hs:FederalFindingsText

element FederalFindingsText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/5.0/

Definition

A court's description of its factual findings supporting conclusions about legal standards defined in federal law. Usage: factual findings regarding an agency's reasonable efforts to reunify a family, a child's placement in the least restrictive placement necessary, etc.
