hs:JuvenileAbuseNeglectAllegation

element JuvenileAbuseNeglectAllegation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.2/

Definition

A set of specifics of the referred incident of abuse or neglect as it relates to the victim. Information recorded includes the Abuse/Neglect Category and Type as well as narrative descriptions of the abuse and/or injuries
