hs:ChildSupportTANFArrearsAmount

element ChildSupportTANFArrearsAmount in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A portion of total arrears identified in the child-support order as being attributable to recovery from the obligor of the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) funds previously provided for the child.
