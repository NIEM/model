hs:ActionRequestedCode

element ActionRequestedCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A code indicating any of the actions being requested of the court e.g. what types of services the child needs (e.g. mental health, medical, emergency, basic, therapeutic, habilitative).
