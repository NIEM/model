hs:NPIIdentification

element NPIIdentification in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/5.0/

Definition

An identification called the National Provider Identifier, which includes a unique 10-digit identification number issued to health care providers in the United States by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS).
