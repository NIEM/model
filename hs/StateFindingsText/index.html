hs:StateFindingsText

element StateFindingsText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/5.0/

Definition

A court's description of its factual findings supporting conclusions about legal standards defined in state law. Usage: factual findings regarding dependency, neglect, abuse, danger to self or others, or a child's need for emergency, etc.
