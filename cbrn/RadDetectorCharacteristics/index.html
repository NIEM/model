cbrn:RadDetectorCharacteristics

element RadDetectorCharacteristics in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/5.0/

Definition

A radiation detector's characteristics that are not otherwise explicitly addressed in this standard. Each non-standard characteristic consists of name, value, units, and value data class. Characteristics may also be organized in characteristic groups.
