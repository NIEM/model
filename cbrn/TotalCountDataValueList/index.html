cbrn:TotalCountDataValueList

element TotalCountDataValueList in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/5.0/

Definition

A list of values for the total number of counts accumulated since the last radiation detection instrument reset over the entire energy range measured by the radiation detection instrument or within pre-defined energy windows.
