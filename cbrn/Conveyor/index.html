cbrn:Conveyor

element Conveyor in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/5.0/

Definition

A unique identifier of the conveyance that is the end/target of the relationship. By convention, the end of the relationship is the conveyor and the start of the relationship is the conveyee.
