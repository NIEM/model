cbrn:AverageDoseRateUncertaintyValue

element AverageDoseRateUncertaintyValue in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.0/

Definition

A combined 1-sigma uncertainty associated with an average ambient dose equivalent rate reported in an analysis results, expressed in microsieverts per hour (Sv/h).
